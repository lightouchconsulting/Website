# Accelerating Development in the Age of Generative AI
The rapid evolution of generative AI (Gen AI) is transforming the landscape of software development, presenting both opportunities and challenges for organisations. As CIOs, it is essential to recognise the potential of Gen AI to revolutionise development workflows, improve efficiency, and reduce costs. However, to unlock these benefits, organisations must focus their efforts on harnessing the power of Gen AI, rather than simply adopting it as a novelty.

## The Transformative Power of Gen AI in Development
Gen AI has the potential to be a game-changer in software development, enabling organisations to automate routine tasks, improve code quality, and enhance the overall development experience. By leveraging Gen AI, developers can focus on higher-value tasks, such as designing and implementing complex systems, rather than getting bogged down in mundane coding tasks. Moreover, Gen AI can help organisations to improve the speed and accuracy of development, reducing the time and cost associated with software development. However, to achieve these benefits, organisations must invest in the necessary infrastructure, training, and support to ensure that their development teams can effectively utilise Gen AI.

## The Importance of Human Touch in AI-Driven Development
While Gen AI has the potential to revolutionise software development, it is essential to remember that human developers are still essential to the process. The increasing reliance on AI programming tools has raised concerns among developers about the potential loss of coding skills and the devaluation of human expertise. Organisations must strike a balance between leveraging the power of Gen AI and preserving the skills and expertise of their development teams. This can be achieved by implementing AI-driven tools that augment, rather than replace, human developers, and by providing training and support to help developers work effectively with Gen AI. Furthermore, organisations must also consider the importance of digital experience monitoring in the development workflow, ensuring that the user experience is optimised and that any issues are quickly identified and resolved.

## Your Next Step
To accelerate development in the age of Gen AI, CIOs should take a strategic and focused approach to harnessing the power of this technology. This includes investing in the necessary infrastructure and training to support Gen AI adoption, implementing AI-driven tools that augment human developers, and prioritising digital experience monitoring in the development workflow. By taking these steps, organisations can unlock the full potential of Gen AI, improve the efficiency and effectiveness of their development teams, and deliver high-quality software solutions that meet the evolving needs of their customers. As a first step, CIOs should conduct a thorough assessment of their current development workflows and identify areas where Gen AI can be leveraged to drive innovation and improvement, setting the stage for a successful and sustainable adoption of this transformative technology.
